University of Pennsylvania Engineering

Penn Engineering offers students the theoretical foundation and practical instruction needed to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. With a beautiful campus located in the heart of Philadelphia, our students have the unique opportunity to study in an Ivy League, multidisciplinary environment with access to top-tier research facilities.

With 16 master's and 6 doctoral programs, Penn Engineering's faculty and alumni networks offer unparalleled access to opportunities for our graduates as they go on to careers in industry or academia. Visit our website to learn how Penn Engineering can help you to succeed: https://gradadm.seas.upenn.edu/

Virginia Tech Graduate School

The Virginia Tech Innovation Campus will be both a place and a culture that unlocks the power of diverse people and ideas to solve the world’s most pressing problems through technology. We have set the goal to be the most diverse graduate tech program in the country. Masters degrees in Computer Science and Computer Engineering will include project based learning and strong industry and government partnerships. Accelerated Bachelor's to Masters (4+1) programs are available to fast track the degree. Apply at vt.edu/masters and send questions to techtalent@vt.edu.
